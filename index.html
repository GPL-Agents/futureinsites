Approach

Strategy first.

FutureInSites was built on a simple belief: the companies that win with AI aren't the ones who deploy the most tools; they're the ones who ask the right questions first.

We do two complementary kinds of work. Enterprise AI Strategy for operating companies navigating the AI inflection point, and AI Investment & Strategic Diligence for investors and strategic teams evaluating AI businesses and AI's impact on incumbent economics.

The case work covers retail, media & technology, finance, healthcare and bio-sciences: industries where AI is reshaping customer expectations, capital intensity, and competitive advantage simultaneously.

Solutions

What we do

From readiness assessment to full implementation roadmap, our engagements are structured around where you actually are, and where AI can generate the fastest, most defensible returns.

AI Readiness Assessment

A structured audit of your data infrastructure, talent, culture, and existing AI investments. Outputs include a capability heatmap, gap analysis, and a clear-eyed view of where you can move now versus what needs to be built first.

AI Strategy & Roadmap

A prioritized, phased AI strategy tied directly to your business objectives. Includes use case prioritization across a value vs. complexity framework, build/buy/partner decisions, governance frameworks, and investment sizing.

Vendor Selection & Architecture

Navigate the AI vendor landscape across model providers, infrastructure, and applied AI software. We help you select, configure, and integrate the right platforms, with reference architectures that protect optionality as the landscape keeps shifting.

AI Governance & Ethics

Establish responsible AI frameworks, stage-gate investment criteria, and Centers of Excellence that scale your AI program without unmanaged risk. Policy guardrails, model risk management, and the operating cadence that keeps Board oversight honest as deployments multiply.

Executive Advisory

Ongoing strategic counsel for C-suite and Board-level AI decision-making. Quarterly strategy reviews, scenario planning around frontier model and capital allocation choices, and a thinking partner for the calls that don't fit cleanly on a slide.

AI Investment & Strategic Diligence

Investment-grade analysis of AI businesses and AI's impact on incumbent economics. Two-scenario DCF modeling, GPU economic depreciation analysis, customer concentration assessment, and forecast frameworks traceable to source disclosures.
