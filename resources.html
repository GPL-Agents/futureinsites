The AI Vendor Landscape

01

Frontier Model Labs

The handful of organizations training the largest, most capable general-purpose models. These labs anchor the cost curve, the safety frontier, and most enterprise AI roadmaps.

OpenAI
openai.com

Maker of the GPT and o-series models, ChatGPT, Sora, and the OpenAI API. Anchored by a deep partnership with Microsoft.
Anthropic
anthropic.com

Creator of Claude, with a research focus on AI safety, interpretability, and building steerable models for high-stakes enterprise work.
Google DeepMind
deepmind.google

Google's combined AI research division behind Gemini, AlphaFold, and Veo. Deeply integrated into Search, Workspace, and Google Cloud.
Meta AI
ai.meta.com

Meta's research arm and home of the Llama family of open-weight models. The reference open-source ecosystem for enterprise self-hosting.
xAI
x.ai

Elon Musk's AI lab behind the Grok model family, integrated across X (formerly Twitter) and exposed via its own API.
Mistral AI
mistral.ai

Paris-based lab producing both open-weight and frontier-tier models with a strong focus on European data sovereignty.
Cohere
cohere.com

Enterprise-focused foundation models (Command, Embed, Rerank) optimized for retrieval-augmented generation in regulated industries.
AI21 Labs
ai21.com

Israeli lab behind the Jamba family of hybrid Mamba/Transformer models, focused on long-context enterprise use cases.
Reka AI
reka.ai

Multimodal foundation model lab (Reka Core, Flash, Edge) targeting on-prem and edge deployment for enterprise.
Inflection AI
inflection.ai

Pivoted to enterprise after its commercial team joined Microsoft. Now offers Inflection-tuned models for regulated workloads.

Open-Source & Specialist Models

Open-weight, niche, or non-Western frontier models. Often selected for cost, control, sovereignty, or specialized capability.

Hugging Face
huggingface.co

The default hub for open-source model weights, datasets, and training tooling. Effectively the GitHub of machine learning.
Databricks (Mosaic)
databricks.com

Data and AI platform with the DBRX open model and Mosaic AI training stack. Strong story for fine-tuning on proprietary data.
Stability AI
stability.ai

Open-source generative media (Stable Diffusion, Stable Audio, Stable Video). The reference open image-generation lineage.
Black Forest Labs
blackforestlabs.ai

Founded by ex-Stability researchers; the FLUX family is a current state-of-the-art open image-generation lineage.
Aleph Alpha
aleph-alpha.com

German foundation model company focused on sovereign AI deployments for European government and regulated enterprise.
DeepSeek
deepseek.com

Chinese lab whose efficient reasoning models reset assumptions on training cost and open-weight performance.
Alibaba Qwen
qwenlm.ai

Alibaba's open-weight Qwen model family, a major non-Western foundation model series with strong multilingual coverage.
Moonshot AI
moonshot.cn

Maker of the Kimi long-context model family. Notable for strong long-document and reasoning benchmarks.
01.AI
01.ai

Founded by Kai-Fu Lee. Maker of the Yi family of open-weight bilingual models targeting English and Chinese workloads.
Adept AI
adept.ai

Action-oriented multimodal models for browser and desktop automation. Now operating largely as part of Amazon.

Hyperscaler & Cloud AI Platforms

Where most enterprises actually consume AI: hyperscalers and cloud platforms that bundle compute, model gardens, training infrastructure, and managed services.

AWS Bedrock & SageMaker
aws.amazon.com

Amazon's managed model garden (Anthropic, Meta, Mistral, Amazon Nova, Titan) plus the SageMaker training and serving stack.
Microsoft Azure AI
azure.microsoft.com

Azure OpenAI Service, Azure AI Foundry, and Copilot Studio. The default enterprise on-ramp for OpenAI models.
Google Vertex AI
cloud.google.com

Google Cloud's AI platform: Gemini, Imagen, Veo, Model Garden, and managed training, all wired into BigQuery and TPU v5/Trillium.
IBM watsonx
ibm.com

IBM's enterprise AI platform built around the Granite model family, governance tooling, and hybrid-cloud deployment.
Oracle Cloud AI (OCI)
oracle.com

OCI Generative AI Service plus Oracle's high-density GPU clusters underpinning much of its AI infrastructure backlog.
Alibaba Cloud
alibabacloud.com

The leading non-US hyperscaler in many APAC markets, with deep integration into the Qwen model family.

Compute, Silicon & Inference

The supply side of AI: silicon, GPU clouds, and inference platforms. The economics here flow downstream into every model and product.

NVIDIA
nvidia.com

The dominant AI accelerator vendor. CUDA, H100/H200/Blackwell, NIM microservices, and the broader Omniverse / DGX stack.
AMD
amd.com

The Instinct MI300/MI350/MI400 GPU line plus ROCm software. The credible second source named in most major AI build-outs.
Intel (Gaudi)
intel.com

Intel's Gaudi accelerator line plus Xeon AI extensions. A challenger position with selective enterprise traction.
Cerebras
cerebras.net

Wafer-scale AI engines targeting the largest single-system training and ultra-fast inference workloads.
Groq
groq.com

Custom LPU inference silicon delivering very high tokens-per-second economics for production LLM serving.
SambaNova
sambanova.ai

Reconfigurable dataflow accelerators packaged as a full enterprise AI stack, often deployed on-prem.
Tenstorrent
tenstorrent.com

Open-architecture AI silicon (Wormhole, Blackhole) with a RISC-V CPU strategy led by Jim Keller.
Graphcore
graphcore.ai

UK-based IPU silicon vendor, now part of SoftBank. Refocused on next-generation AI compute architectures.
CoreWeave
coreweave.com

Pure-play GPU hyperscaler. The reference example of NVIDIA-aligned, AI-native cloud capacity at scale.
Lambda Labs
lambdalabs.com

GPU cloud and on-prem AI infrastructure. Popular with research labs and mid-market AI-native companies.
Crusoe
crusoe.ai

Vertically integrated AI cloud building dedicated power and data centers, including stranded-energy and gas-to-AI sites.
Together AI
together.ai

Inference and fine-tuning cloud for open-source models. Often the cheapest production path for Llama-class workloads.
Fireworks AI
fireworks.ai

High-performance inference platform with strong throughput on open-weight LLMs and multimodal models.
Modal
modal.com

Serverless GPU compute with a Python-first developer experience. Popular for batch inference and fine-tuning jobs.
RunPod
runpod.io

On-demand and spot GPU rental targeted at developers, hobbyists, and AI-native startups.
Anyscale
anyscale.com

Commercial Ray distribution. The reference framework for distributed training, RL, and large-scale inference workloads.

The plumbing layer underneath production AI: vector databases, retrieval, observability, evaluation, and the orchestration stack.

Pinecone
pinecone.io

Managed vector database widely used for RAG. The reference enterprise serverless vector store.
Weaviate
weaviate.io

Open-source vector database with strong hybrid search and graph features for retrieval-augmented applications.

High-performance open-source vector database written in Rust, popular for self-hosted and on-prem RAG.

Developer-friendly embeddings database; the default choice for prototype RAG pipelines and notebooks.

Commercial offering and steward of Milvus, the large-scale open-source vector database used heavily in APAC.
LanceDB
lancedb.com

Embedded vector database built on the Lance columnar format. Often used inside multimodal data pipelines.
LangChain & LangSmith
langchain.com

Open-source orchestration framework plus a hosted observability and evaluation product (LangSmith) for LLM applications.
LlamaIndex
llamaindex.ai

Data framework specialized in RAG and agentic document workflows. The other dominant open-source orchestration choice.
Weights & Biases
wandb.ai

The dominant ML experiment-tracking and observability platform. Widely used inside frontier labs and enterprise ML teams.
Comet ML
comet.com

End-to-end ML and LLM experiment management with a strong story around model registry and production monitoring.
Arize AI
arize.com

Production ML and LLM observability, with the open-source Phoenix tooling and a hosted enterprise monitoring product.
Fiddler
fiddler.ai

Model observability and explainability for regulated industries. Strong story around bias, drift, and audit.
Galileo
galileo.ai

LLM evaluation, hallucination detection, and guardrails specifically designed for production GenAI applications.
Humanloop
humanloop.com

LLM evaluation and prompt management platform. Targets product teams shipping LLM features at scale.
Snowflake Cortex
snowflake.com

Snowflake's LLM and ML feature set, bringing model serving to the data warehouse rather than moving data to the model.
Scale AI
scale.com

Data labeling, evaluation, and RLHF infrastructure. A core supplier to frontier labs and the US public sector.
